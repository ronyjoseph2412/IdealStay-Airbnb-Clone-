All-Inclusive: The Natural Adventure.


We start with a pick-up at Lisbon Zoo and head south to Arrabida’s Natural Park, where we stop by my family's farm house to suit up, grab all the food and amenities to make sure we are always good, warm and refreshed and have a snack/coffee before driving to the beach. We head over to Portinho’s Bay Creiro Beach, an old pirate bay full of incredible secrets, and our adventure begins! We kayak along the beautiful blue coast to several caves, a unique place with old legends and great cliffs, we go for some dives and a stop for snacks and drinks so we can recharge our energy. We find amazing caves naturally lit with magical “moon doors”, a zoological marine reserve island where we snorkel with thousands of fish and look for starfish in the cristal clear waters. After all the workout, lunch back at the beach, a picnic full of goodies as we definitely “earned it”. Afterwards we drive back to my parent’s farm and reach the highest points of Arrabida mountain for some breathtaking panoramic views and pictures. In the evening we will be enjoying homemade port wine and grape juice by the pool to an astonishing view over the Valleys and Hills..
