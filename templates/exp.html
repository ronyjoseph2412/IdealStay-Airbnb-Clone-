Berlin, Germany. Explore the Graffiti City by Bike.

Right in the heart of Berlin you’ll find a sweet little street art hotspot. Tucked away in a little alley off Rosenthaler Strasse near Alexanderplatz, you’ll find Haus Schwarzenberg. Owned by a non-profit art organisation, this vibrant cultural pocket is one of the best places to see street art in Berlin.
Quite surprising actually since it’s located in a more commercial shopping district. looking into the alley of Haus Schwarzenberg on Rosenthaler Strasse which is covered in street art.

And lastly, this one. Coimbatore,India.

Though most simply pass through Coimbatore on their way west, tarriers are rewarded with a lively street-food scene, city museums, and parks. Add in easy access to the hilltop temples surrounding the industrial city, and you have a raw gem waiting to be discovered.

Coimbatore, Known as “THE MANCHESTER OF SOUTH INDIA” is a well known city in India. It is reputed for its pleasant climate and the warm nature. It is one of the top most Industrial cities of India.
